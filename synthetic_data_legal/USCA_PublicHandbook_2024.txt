Public Handbook: Fraud Offenses (Educational Summary)
This handbook is a simplified overview intended for public education and compliance training.
Financial Fraud
California law includes penalties that may reach up to 3 years under revised provisions, depending on the effective date and charge.
Identity Theft
Some California identity theft statutes provide for penalties up to 5 years under updated versions.
Benefits Fraud
Some California benefits fraud provisions may provide penalties up to 2 years under later revisions.
