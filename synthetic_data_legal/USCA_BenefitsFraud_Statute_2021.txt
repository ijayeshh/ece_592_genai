Section 1 — Scope
This statute updates California benefits fraud provisions to address repeated misreporting and digital submissions.
Section 2 — Definitions
•	Digital submission includes online forms and electronic verification. 
•	Material means likely to influence eligibility. 
Section 3 — Elements
Benefits fraud occurs when a person knowingly makes a material misrepresentation or omission to obtain benefits.
Section 4 — Penalty (Revised)
If convicted under this statute, the court may impose:
•	Imprisonment up to 2 years, and/or 
•	a fine up to $25,000, and/or 
•	restitution.
