Section 1 — Scope
This statute defines benefits fraud for knowingly providing false information to obtain government benefits or payments.
Section 2 — Definitions
•	Benefits include public assistance payments and subsidized programs. 
•	False statement includes omissions where disclosure is required. 
Section 3 — Elements
Benefits fraud occurs when a person knowingly makes a material false statement or omits a required fact to obtain benefits.
Section 4 — Penalty
If convicted under this statute, the court may impose:
•	Imprisonment up to 9 months, and/or 
•	a fine up to $15,000, and/or 
•	restitution.
