Overview Bulletin (Non-binding)
This bulletin summarizes common California enforcement themes for fraud-related crimes. It is not itself a statute.
Common Summaries (as of publication date)
•	Financial fraud was frequently summarized as exposure “up to 1 year” under then-current state provisions in typical cases. 
•	Identity theft was often summarized as exposure “up to 2 years” under then-current state provisions. 
•	Benefits fraud was often summarized as exposure “up to 6 months” in routine cases, depending on charged provisions. 
Important Note
California statutes may be amended. If a later statutory revision applies, the statutory maximum in that revision controls.
