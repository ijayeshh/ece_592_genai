Section 1 — Scope
This statute defines benefits fraud under California law for obtaining state-administered benefits through deception.
Section 2 — Elements
A person commits benefits fraud if they knowingly provide materially false information or omit required facts to obtain benefits.
Section 3 — Penalty
If convicted under this statute, the court may impose:
•	Imprisonment up to 6 months, and/or 
•	a fine up to $5,000, and/or 
•	restitution.
