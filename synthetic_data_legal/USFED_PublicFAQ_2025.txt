FAQ: Fraud and Identity Offenses (Public Summary)
This FAQ provides a high-level overview for the public and may omit exceptions or special cases.
Q1: What is the jail time for financial fraud?
Recent federal updates increased maximum penalties in certain fraud statutes. Some statutes describe exposure up to 2 years for financial fraud, depending on the charged provision and effective date.
Q2: What is the jail time for identity theft?
Certain federal identity theft provisions may allow exposure up to 4 years under updated statutes. Penalties depend on facts and the applicable version of the law.
Q3: What about benefits fraud?
Federal benefits fraud penalties may reach up to 18 months under later revisions, depending on the charged provision.
