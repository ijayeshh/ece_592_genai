Jurisdiction: US-FED
Authority: Statute (authority_rank=3)
Effective date: 2022-10-01
Section 1 — Scope
This statute revises federal benefits fraud provisions, including enforcement against repeated misreporting and coordinated claims.
Section 2 — Definitions
•	Material means likely to influence eligibility determination. 
•	Coordinated claims include multiple submissions using shared identifiers. 
Section 3 — Elements
Benefits fraud occurs when a person knowingly makes a material misrepresentation or omission to obtain benefits.
Section 4 — Penalty (Revised)
If convicted under this statute, the court may impose:
•	Imprisonment up to 18 months, and/or 
•	a fine up to $40,000, and/or 
•	restitution.
